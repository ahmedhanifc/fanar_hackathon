This foundational law is the bedrock of Qatar's legal stance on cybercrime. It criminalizes unauthorized access, hacking, data breaches, and other cyber offenses.

Example Articles:

Article 2: Any person who unlawfully accesses an information system shall be punished with imprisonment for a term not exceeding three years, and/or a fine not exceeding QR 500,000.

Article 3: Any person who intentionally disables, damages, or destroys an information system or data shall be punished with imprisonment for a term not exceeding ten years, and/or a fine not exceeding QR 2,000,000.
